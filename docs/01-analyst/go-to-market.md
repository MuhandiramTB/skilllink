# Go-To-Market Strategy — SkillLink LK

**Owner:** Analyst/PM · **Consumed by:** Owner, Marketing

## Sequencing
Two-sided marketplace → seed **providers first** (supply), then drive **customers** (demand)
in the same pilot city to keep matches fast.

## Provider acquisition channels
- Facebook groups & WhatsApp trade communities
- Hardware stores & vehicle repair shops (point-of-sale flyers/QR)
- Technical colleges & vocational training centers
- Trade associations
- **Solar:** solar installer networks, EPC subcontractors, EV-charger vendors

## Customer acquisition channels
- Facebook ads (geo-targeted to pilot city)
- TikTok short-form (before/after job demos, multilingual)
- Referral program (credit for both sides)
- Local partnerships (apartment complexes, offices)
- SEO (category + city landing pages, trilingual)

## Pilot focus
City: Colombo / Gampaha / Kandy. Categories: Electricians, Plumbers, AC technicians.
Targets: 100 providers, 1,000 customers, 500 completed jobs.

## Solar GTM (Phase 4)
Position solar as higher-value services; partner with solar vendors; content on
net-metering & EV chargers; capture system specs to improve quote accuracy.

## Messaging
"Find verified skilled professionals near your location within minutes." — trust,
proximity, multilingual, transparent pricing.
