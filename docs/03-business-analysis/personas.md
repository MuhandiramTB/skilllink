# User Personas — SkillLink LK

**Owner:** Business Analyst · **Consumed by:** PM, UX, QA

---

## P1 — Nimal (Customer / Homeowner)
- **Age/Location:** 38, Colombo 02. Sinhala-first, reads some English.
- **Device:** Mid-range Android on mobile data.
- **Goal:** Get a trustworthy electrician/plumber/AC tech fast, at a fair price.
- **Frustrations:** Relies on Facebook groups & WhatsApp; can't tell who's reliable; no price clarity.
- **Needs:** verified providers, quick OTP login, photo-based issue description, transparent price, reviews.

## P2 — Priya (Customer / Overseas owner)
- **Age/Location:** 45, Melbourne; manages family home in Kandy.
- **Goal:** Hire & pay a provider remotely for a relative's home.
- **Frustrations:** Can't be on-site; needs proof of completion and remote payment.
- **Needs:** remote booking, job-completion photos, secure online payment, live tracking, English UI.

## P3 — Sunil (Provider / Electrician)
- **Age/Location:** 32, Gampaha. Sinhala/Tamil.
- **Goal:** Steady stream of nearby jobs and a good reputation.
- **Frustrations:** Fake inquiries, no digital presence, irregular work.
- **Needs:** easy onboarding, NIC/cert verification, service-radius control, availability toggle, earnings view, review management.

## P4 — Fathima (Provider / Solar technician)
- **Age/Location:** 29, Negombo. Tamil/English.
- **Goal:** Win higher-value solar jobs (install, inverter repair, EV chargers).
- **Frustrations:** Customers don't capture system specs → bad quotes.
- **Needs:** declare solar certs & equipment, see system details (kW, brand) before quoting.

## P5 — Ayesha (Admin / Operations)
- **Age/Location:** 27, head office.
- **Goal:** Keep the marketplace trustworthy and running.
- **Frustrations:** Manual verification, disputes, fraud.
- **Needs:** verification queue, dispute tools, analytics/revenue dashboards, fraud flags.

> **Solar note:** the platform must serve both P4 (solar provider) and solar-buying
> variants of P1/P2. Solar flows are higher-value and spec-sensitive.
