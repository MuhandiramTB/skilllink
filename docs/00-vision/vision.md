# Product Vision — SkillLink LK

**Owner:** Business Owner / Founder
**Status:** Approved
**Last updated:** 2026-06-19

## Vision statement
> Become Sri Lanka's leading on-demand skilled-services marketplace by letting any
> customer find a *verified*, *nearby*, *fairly-priced* professional within minutes —
> in their own language.

## The problem (validated in the research doc)
**Customers** struggle to find reliable providers; they rely on Facebook/WhatsApp/word-of-mouth,
with no transparent pricing, quality guarantee, or easy comparison.
**Providers** struggle to find customers, lack digital presence and a reputation system,
and waste time on fake inquiries.

## The bet
A trust-centric, location-based, multilingual marketplace ("Uber for skilled workers")
that nationally out-covers Colombo-centric incumbents (Blu.lk, Fixie.lk, SourceTradesman,
Waddo, FindMe).

## Strategic pillars
1. **Trust** — verification, reviews, call masking, secure payments, warranties.
2. **Coverage** — nationwide, multilingual (Sinhala/Tamil/English), beyond urban areas.
3. **Speed** — real-time geo-matching and emergency support.

## Success metrics (North Star + supporting)
- **North Star:** Completed bookings per week.
- MAU (customers & providers), avg. provider response time, customer & provider retention,
  CSAT, revenue per booking, verified-provider ratio.

## Funding / phase gates (owner decisions)
| Gate | Trigger | Decision |
|------|---------|----------|
| G0 → G1 | 100+ interested users, 30+ verified providers (Phase 0) | Approve MVP build |
| G1 → G2 | Working PWA + admin dashboard (Phase 2) | Approve pilot |
| G2 → G3 | Pilot: 100 providers, 1,000 customers, 500 jobs | Approve scale |
| G3 → G4 | Product-market fit signals | Approve native apps |

## Revenue model (start with commission)
Primary: **10–15% commission per completed booking.** Later: lead fees, provider
subscriptions, advertising, warranty fees. (Detail in [docs/08-management/business-model.md](../08-management/business-model.md).)

## Long-term vision
Expand from home & vehicle services into construction, cleaning, and freelance
technical work — a complete service ecosystem for Sri Lanka.
