# Business Model & Metrics — SkillLink LK

**Owner:** Business Owner

## Revenue streams (sequenced)
1. **Commission (launch):** 10–15% per completed booking. Computed server-side, stored as `commission_cents`.
2. **Lead fee (optional):** providers pay per delivered lead.
3. **Subscriptions (Phase 4):** verified badge, higher ranking, more leads, analytics.
4. **Advertising (Phase 4):** hardware/tool/insurance partners.
5. **Warranty fees (Phase 4):** extended service guarantees.

## Unit economics (to validate in pilot)
- Avg. booking value (LKR), commission per booking, CAC (customer & provider), payback period.

## North Star & KPIs
| Metric | Target trend |
|--------|--------------|
| Completed bookings / week (North Star) | ↑ |
| MAU (customers & providers) | ↑ |
| Avg. provider response time | ↓ |
| Customer retention | ↑ |
| Provider retention | ↑ |
| CSAT | ≥ 4.3/5 |
| Verified-provider ratio | ↑ |
| Revenue per booking | stable/↑ |

## Go-to-market (from research)
- **Provider acquisition:** FB groups, WhatsApp communities, hardware stores, repair shops, technical colleges, trade associations.
- **Customer acquisition:** Facebook ads, TikTok, referrals, local partnerships, SEO.
